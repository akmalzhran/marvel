Welcome to the Marvel Universe!

Marvel: A Superhero World That Inspires the World

Marvel is a big name in the entertainment world, especially known for its superhero universe rich in characters, stories, and heroic values. Founded in 1939 as Timely Publications and later changed to Marvel Comics in 1961, the company has created legendary icons such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, and many more. Marvel's success is not only due to its interesting characters, but also because of the interconnected storylines, especially in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The MCU began in 2008 with the film Iron Man, and has since grown into one of the biggest film franchises of all time. Films like The Avengers, Infinity War, and Endgame have not only broken box office records, but have also touched the emotions of millions of viewers around the world. One of Marvel's main strengths is its ability to present stories that are not only full of action, but also touch on human issues such as responsibility, sacrifice, friendship, and hope. Marvel superheroes are not depicted as perfect beings, but as ordinary people who struggle with their flaws and moral dilemmas. Today, Marvel continues to grow by introducing new generations of heroes through films, TV series, and other media. With a spirit of innovation and bold storytelling, Marvel remains a symbol of strength, hope, and inspiration for fans around the world.
